# OneHealth EU: Health Education Comparative Analysis

Welcome to the public research portal for the **OneHealth EU** project. This initiative analyzes how European Union member states integrate **One Health (OH)** and **Planetary Health (PH)** concepts into their national health education curricula.

## Project Scope
- **Comparative Analysis**: 27 EU Member States.
- **Thematic Focus**: Sustainable Diets, Climate Change, Zoonoses, and Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR).
- **Target Audience**: Researchers, policy-makers, and educational experts.

## Key Resources
- [Expert Reviews](reviews/ES.md): Validation from local public health and education experts.
- [Document Registry](registry.md): Direct access to standardized national documents.
- [Data Analysis](dashboard.md): Interactive visualization of OH/PH concepts in current curricula.
